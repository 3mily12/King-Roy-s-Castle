Play through a day in the life of King Roy, the monarch of a world in total darkness!

  King Roy's Castle is a simple escape room that walks the player through rediscovering Roy's old life despite his dementia. This game was made in a week on Bitsy for DIG4715C. Any feedback is welcome!
